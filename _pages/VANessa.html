VANessa: Virtual Assistant for Neurodivergent Energy-Sensitive Scheduling Automation

Smart scheduling for humans, not robots.

A custom-built scheduling assistant designed not just for productivity — but for neurodivergent survival in academia.

Meet VANessa:

VANessa (Virtual Assistant for Neurodivergent Energy-Sensitive Scheduling Automation — yes, really) is a custom Python-based scheduling system I designed and built from the ground up.
She’s been developed to help manage cognitive load, balance realistic energy levels, and prevent the kind of burnout that too often comes with high-pressure academic life.

Think Reclaim.ai or Motion — but smarter, spoon-aware and built entirely by one exhausted post-PhD brain with a mission.

How It Works

Subpoints: Task Synchronization: VANessa pulls in tasks from your Todoist (or other task manager), schedules them into your calendar, and adjusts based on your energy levels (thanks to integrations with energy tracking). Flexible Scheduling: Works around your mood, productivity peaks, and low-energy periods, making sure you’re not pushed to overwork during down times. Smart Prioritization: Uses an AI-powered scoring system to figure out the most important tasks and intelligently shifts them around when new ones are added. No more endless manual rescheduling. Behavioral Learning: Learns how you work over time and adapts, so the more you use it, the smarter she gets.

Tech Behind the Magic

Think of VANessa as the product of Python wizardry, a sprinkle of AI smarts, and solid integrations with tools you already use.
Here’s what powers her:

Tech Stack:

  • Python: The backbone, bringing in task automation and logic handling.
  • Flask: Used to create the lightweight web app that houses the scheduling engine.
  • Google Cloud: For deploying and scaling the app securely and with reliability.
  • Todoist API: For fetching tasks, managing priorities, and adding/editing tasks seamlessly.
  • AI-powered Scheduling: Leveraging reinforcement learning to refine how tasks are scored and rescheduled, improving over time.
